\# Major Household Appliance Brands – United States



This file lists common appliance brands sold in the US for major household products.



Main category groups:



\- \*\*Cooking\*\*: cooktops, ranges, ovens, microwaves  

\- \*\*Cooling\*\*: refrigerators, freezers, ice machines, room air conditioners (RAC), beverage centers, wine coolers  

\- \*\*Cleaning\*\*:

&nbsp; - laundry: washers, dryers

&nbsp; - dish: dishwashers



Each brand entry:



\- \*\*primary\_name\*\*

\- \*\*aliases\*\*

\- \*\*parent\_company\*\*

\- \*\*categories\*\* – which of the above groups they sell in the US

\- \*\*market\_position\*\* – simple text

About Wizard Knows

Wizard Knows is a focused helper for major home appliances. It’s built to give you calm, step‑by‑step guidance, and to keep you away from repairs that belong in the hands of a qualified technician.

What Wizard Knows can help with

Wizard is designed around common household appliances, including:

  • Refrigerators and freezers
  • Washers and dryers
  • Ranges, ovens, and cooktops
  • Dishwashers
  • Water heaters and basic HVAC issues

Safety first, always

Wizard is deliberately conservative about what it will walk you through. It avoids:

  • Sealed‑system refrigeration work
  • Bypassing safety devices or interlocks
  • High‑risk gas or high‑voltage electrical diagnostics

You’ll see clear warnings whenever a step might involve shock, fire, flood, or other real‑world risk, and Wizard will encourage you to call a professional when something goes beyond safe DIY.

How the knowledge base fits in

Behind the scenes, Wizard Knows is gradually being connected to a structured knowledge base written for specific products and cabinet styles—for example, top‑mount refrigerators with known symptoms and repair paths.

Over time, more appliances and symptom trees will be added, so Wizard can give more precise, model‑aware guidance instead of generic advice.

Contact and feedback

If something doesn’t look right, or if you have a suggestion about how Wizard could explain a step more clearly, you can reach us at wizardknows@outlook.com.

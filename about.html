About WizardKnows

Today's products don't last the way they used to. Most major appliances come with only a one-year warranty, and many start breaking down after just 5â€“6 years. We're expected to live with frequent failures, costly service calls, and a constant cycle of replacement. That's hard on our wallets, and even harder on the environment.

WizardKnows.com was created to push back against that cycle.

We help everyday DIYers and curious consumers understand how their appliances and household products actually work, and how to repair them when something goes wrong. Instead of sending a "broken" product straight to the curb, you can get clear explanations, troubleshooting guidance, and step-by-step help.

At the core of the site is Wizard, an AI-powered repair guide designed to turn confusing technical issues into plain language you can act on. Wizard helps you diagnose common problems, explore safe repair options, and decide when you can handle it yourself and when you truly need a professional.

Every time you repair instead of replace, you give an appliance a second life instead of a shallow grave. Most of these products will never be meaningfully recycled; they're buried, abandoned, or left to rust as wasted material. By keeping them working longer, we cut down on needless manufacturing, wasted money, and the constant flow of "junk" to landfills and empty lots.

Manufacturers often promote energy efficiency, but true sustainability also means durability, repairability, and honest information. WizardKnows stands with consumers who want products that work, last, and don't have to be thrown away so quickly.

Our Mission

To empower people to understand, maintain, and repair the products they own. Let's extend their lifespan, save money, and fight the throwaway culture that buries usable materials in landfills instead of keeping them in everyday use.
